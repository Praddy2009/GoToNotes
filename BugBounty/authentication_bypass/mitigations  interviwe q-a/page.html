Mitigations
  • Que: What is the fix?
  • Do not rely on client side, Checks at server side end are important.
  • Verifying at the client side only & taking decisions is dangerous
  • Use Authentication based on strong Tokens JWT(JSON web token) Mechanism
  • Use authentication based on encrypted data which can be AES for example.
